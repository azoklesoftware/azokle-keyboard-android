# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026

At [Azokle Software](https://azokle.com), we believe your right to private, unmonitored communication is absolute. **Azokle Keyboard** is engineered with a strict minimal-data philosophy.

## 1. Zero Telemetry & Zero Server
Azokle Keyboard is a serverless application. It **does not connect to the internet**, and we do not provide backends, tracking servers, telemetry services, or analytics dashboards. 

## 2. On-Device Encryption 
All encryption keys, contacts, message histories, and UUID identities are generated locally and securely stored inside your Android device's isolated SharedPreferences sandbox. We do not and cannot access this data.

## 3. Sandboxed Architecture
The application purposefully requests only a single baseline permission (`VIBRATE`) to provide haptic feedback during typing. It does **not** request access to your device's global Contact Book, Storage, Network, or Location.

To read our overarching corporate privacy alignments and open-source policies, please visit: [policies.azokle.com/privacy](https://policies.azokle.com/privacy/).
